About Me

I am a Canadian web developer based in Sydney looking for my next exciting opportunity. Currently I am studying full time at Coder Academy's full stack web development program. In my spare time, I am working on a side project, hanging out at Meetups (JS and Blockchain) or soaking in the beautiful scenery around town.

I believe technology can be a powerful tool to create a better world. I am especially interested in exploring blockchain technologies, cryptocurrencies, and decentralized applications.

In my previous career, I was a real estate appraiser in the public sector for six years. Crunching data, building valuation models and supporting my market estimation was my daily routine. After years in the real estate industry, I am looking to help people in other ways and at greater scale by leveraging technology. I looking forward to working with you. Let’s build something great together.
